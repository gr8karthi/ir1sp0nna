Non-Destructive Testing is the branch of engineering concerned with all methods of detecting and evaluating flaws in materials. Flaws affecting the serviceability of the material or structure, so NDT are important in guaranteeing safe operation as well as in quality control and assessing plant life. The flaws may be cracks or inclusions in welds and castings or variations in structure properties that can lead to loss of strength or failure in service. The essential feature of NDT is that the test process itself produces no deleterious effect on the materials or structure under stress
