ANST 9712 Certification Program

Certified by ASNT Certification

Global Recognition:

The ASNT 9712 Program offers certification for NDT personnel through examinations, adhering to the process established by the globally recognized body, ASNT Certification.

ISO Standard Compliance

Designed to fulfill the requirements of ISO Standard 9712:2021, ensuring up-to-date industry practices.

Training by IRIS

As an Authorized Training Centre (ATC), IRIS provides comprehensive training for ASNT 9712 Level II and Level III at its facility in Trichy.

Examination Venue

Certification examinations are conducted at the ASNT India facility located in Chennai.

    ASNT 9712 Level – III

    The Level III certification in the ASNT 9712 program encompasses a comprehensive examination process that includes:

    Basic Examination:

    This evaluates the candidate’s understanding of certification processes, materials, processes, and a general knowledge of various NDT methods.
    Non-destructive testing — Qualification and certification of NDT personnel. NDT Methods: RT, UT, MT, PT.

    Basic Supplemental Examination:

    It focuses on detailed knowledge of examination requirements as per ISO 9712:2021 standards.

    Method Examination:

    This tests the candidate’s in-depth knowledge of the specific NDT method they are being certified in.

    Practical Examination:

    Candidates must demonstrate proficiency in performing the NDT method on test samples with known defects.

    Procedure Preparation Examination:

    Candidates are tasked with creating an NDT procedure based on a provided NDT standard.
